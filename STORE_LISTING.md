### Tips for Best Results

- Generate at higher resolution (512x512 or 1024x1024) and scale down
- Request PNG with transparent background
- Try multiple variations and pick the best
- May need manual touch-up in Figma/Photoshop for pixel-perfect small sizes
- Test icon visibility against both light and dark browser themes
